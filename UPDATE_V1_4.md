# Srpski Tajkun multi-map v1.4

- Home-screen map previews are approximately half their former size.
- Rent-free trade conditions offer 1, 2, 3, 4 or 5 circles.
- The server now increments a player's circle counter whenever that player reaches/passes START, fixing conditions that previously remained active indefinitely.
- A direct card move to START also counts as reaching START.
- Expired rent-free agreements are removed and logged.
- Active trade conditions are displayed compactly inside each affected player's card, including remaining circles.
